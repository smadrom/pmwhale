# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want pmwhale to be a welcoming, technically rigorous community. Be
respectful, assume good intent, criticize ideas rather than people, and make
space for participants with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized
attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, publishing another
person's private information, or pressuring people to reveal wallet ownership or
identity.

## Scope

This standard applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily
or permanently restrict participation when behavior puts the community at risk.

## Reporting and enforcement

Do not report conduct incidents in a public issue. Contact the maintainer
privately at `sn.one.dev@gmail.com`. Reports will be
reviewed promptly, kept as confidential as reasonably possible, and handled with
attention to the safety of affected people. Retaliation against a reporter is not
tolerated.

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this standard
consistently and in good faith.
